Fluffy Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Baking the Pancakes

    1. Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well to ensure even distribution.
    2. Step 2: In another bowl, crack the egg and beat it lightly. Add the milk and melted butter. Stir until the mixture is smooth.
    3. Step 3: Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Use a spatula or whisk to gently fold the mixture until just combined. Do not overmix; it's okay if there are some lumps.

  2. Cooking the Pancakes

    1. Step 4: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter or cooking spray to prevent sticking.
    2. Step 5: Once the skillet is hot, pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Use a ladle or measuring cup to ensure consistent size.
    3. Step 6: Cook the pancakes until bubbles form on the surface and the edges start to look set, about 2-3 minutes.
    4. Step 7: Carefully flip the pancakes using a spatula. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown on both sides.

  3. Serving the Pancakes

    1. Step 8: Once cooked, transfer the pancakes to a serving plate.
    2. Step 9: Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

Cooking Time: Approximately 20 minutes

Servings: 4
