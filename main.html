Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients: Flour, Milk, Eggs, Baking Powder, Sugar, Salt, Butter

Instructions: Mix dry ingredients, then add wet ingredients. Cook on a greased skillet until golden brown on both sides.

Servings: 4

Cheese Omelette

Ingredients: Eggs, Cheese, Salt, Pepper, Butter

Instructions: Beat eggs with salt and pepper, pour into a hot skillet with melted butter. Add cheese, fold, and cook until set.

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 1
