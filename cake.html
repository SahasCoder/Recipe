Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Mixing the Dry Ingredients

    1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
    2. Step 2: In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix well to combine.

  2. Mixing the Wet Ingredients

    1. Step 3: In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, then add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

  3. Combining the Ingredients

    1. Step 4: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Do not overmix.
    2. Step 5: Carefully add the boiling water to the batter, stirring until smooth. The batter will be thin, but this is normal.

  4. Baking the Cake

    1. Step 6: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
    2. Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  5. Cooling and Serving

    1. Step 8: Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
    2. Step 9: Once cooled, frost the cake with your favorite frosting and serve.

Cooking Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 8
