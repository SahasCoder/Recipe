Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cooking the Spaghetti

    1. Step 1: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

  2. Preparing the Sauce

    1. Step 2: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with chopped onion and minced garlic until the beef is cooked through and the onion is translucent.
    2. Step 3: Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning to the skillet. Stir well to combine and let it simmer for about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Serving the Dish

    1. Step 4: Serve the meat sauce over the cooked spaghetti.

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4
