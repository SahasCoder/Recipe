Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Oil

Instructions:

  1. Preparing the Ingredients

    1. Step 1: Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

  2. Cooking the Vegetables

    1. Step 2: Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet. Saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    2. Step 3: Add mixed vegetables to the skillet. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5-7 minutes.

  3. Adding the Sauce

    1. Step 4: Add soy sauce to the vegetables and stir well to combine. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 2
