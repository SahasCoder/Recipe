Grilled Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

Instructions:

  1. Preparing the Chicken

    1. Step 1: Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper.
    2. Step 2: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
    3. Step 3: Grill the chicken breast for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.

  2. Assembling the Sandwich

    1. Step 4: Toast the bread slices if desired.
    2. Step 5: Spread mayonnaise and mustard on the bread slices.
    3. Step 6: Place the grilled chicken breast on one slice of bread.
    4. Step 7: Add lettuce, tomato slices, and cheese on top of the chicken.
    5. Step 8: Top with the other slice of bread to complete the sandwich.

Cooking Time: Approximately 15 minutes

Servings: 1
