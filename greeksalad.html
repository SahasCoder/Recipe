Greek Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Red Onion
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olives
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Chop the lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onion.
  2. Step 2: Mix the chopped vegetables with crumbled feta cheese and olives.
  3. Step 3: Dress with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 2
